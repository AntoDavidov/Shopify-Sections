## 🚀 How to Install a Section

1. Go to your Shopify Admin → **Online Store → Themes**
2. Click **Edit code** on your active theme
3. Under `sections/`, click **Add a new section**
4. Paste the `.liquid` file contents and save
5. Go to **Customize** and add the section to any page

## 🧩 Section Details

### AD - Sticky Split Images (`ad-sticky-text-banner.liquid`)
A visually striking section with two side-by-side images and a sticky heading that stays visible as users scroll.

**Settings:**
- Upload separate desktop & mobile images for both left and right sides
- Choose which image shows on mobile (left, right, or both)
- Control section height, text position, heading size & color

### AD - About us (`ad-about-us.liquid`)
A premium storytelling About Us section designed for fashion, lifestyle, and brand-driven Shopify stores. It combines immersive hero media, founder storytelling, sticky narrative blocks, value cards, and a closing call-to-action into one polished section.

**Highlights:**
- Supports either a hero image or autoplaying hero video
- Includes editable hero kicker, title, rich text, and pronunciation label
- Animated marquee text section for brand messaging
- Founder intro area with image, heading, kicker, and rich text
- Sticky storytelling layout with three customizable story steps
- Repeating balance/principles cards powered by section blocks
- Split content area with image + rich text for deeper brand meaning
- Closing CTA with configurable button label and link
- Built-in reveal animations and reduced-motion accessibility support
- Fully customizable colors for background, text, and muted accents

**Best for:**
- About Us pages
- Brand story / founder story pages
- Premium fashion and lifestyle storefronts
- Landing pages focused on identity and storytelling
